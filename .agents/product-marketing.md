## Competitive Position

**vs. Enterprise IT firms (TCS, Infosys, Wipro):**
- Alentro is MORE responsive, MORE affordable, MORE personal
- Enterprise firms have minimum contract thresholds that exclude SMEs
- Alentro specialises in SME-scale deployments with dedicated account management

**vs. Freelance IT support:**
- Alentro provides 24/7 coverage, SLA guarantees, and a full team vs. a single person
- Structured escalation paths (L1/L2/L3) vs. ad-hoc freelancer availability
- Insurance-backed, professional engagements

**vs. In-house IT team:**
- 60–70% cost saving vs. full-time IT hire (salary + benefits + training + replacement)
- Immediate access to multi-disciplinary expertise (infrastructure + cloud + security)
- No HR overhead or knowledge loss from staff turnover
